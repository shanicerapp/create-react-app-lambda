Bahamas


Take with me a little trip to the Bahamas!


The Bahamas is a group of islands located in the West Indies in the North Atlantic. The official language of the island is English. Furthermore, the Bahamas are quite close, around 2.5h by boat, to Miami which is why many people choose to make a day-trip or weekend trip to the island. Although in most places you can also pay with US Dollars, the official currency of the Bahamas is the Bahamian Dollar and has similar buying power as the US Dollar.


The most famous tourist attraction of the Bahamas are the famous swimming pigs (first picture). I can highly recommend to do a daytrip to one of the private islands with the famous swimming pigs. I did mine with Sandy Toes for $199. Paying this price is worth it since the boat trip, a guided snorkeling tour (second picture), swimming with the pigs, a lunch, and a free cocktail bar are all included in the price. Another trip I would highly recommend is a day trip to Paradise Island and to the Atlantis waterpark. There is even a waterslide which makes you slide through an aquarium with sharks as seen in the third image. Furthermore, going to a little restaurant at the side of the street where all Bahamians go to is an experience to connect with their culture.
